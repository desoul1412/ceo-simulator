# Social Media & Paid Advertising

## Social Media Strategy
- Platform selection based on audience demographics
- Content calendar (30-day minimum)
- Content mix: 70% value, 20% engagement, 10% promotion
- Community management and engagement protocols
- Hashtag strategy and trending topic monitoring

## Paid Advertising
- Channel selection: Google, Facebook/Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok
- Campaign structure: campaigns → ad sets → ads
- Audience targeting and lookalike audiences
- Budget allocation across channels by ROAS
- Creative brief for ad variants
- A/B testing plan (copy, images, audiences)
- Retargeting strategy for cart/signup abandonment

## Reporting
- Weekly: spend, impressions, clicks, conversions
- Monthly: ROAS, CPA, creative performance
- Quarterly: channel allocation review
