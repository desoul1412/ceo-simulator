# Pricing & Conversion

## Pricing Strategy
- **Value-based:** Price based on perceived value to customer, not cost
- **Tiering:** Free / Pro / Enterprise with clear feature differentiation
- **Anchor pricing:** Show highest tier first to anchor expectations
- **Freemium vs trial:** Free forever (limited) vs time-limited full access

## Price Setting Process
1. Research competitor pricing (Tavily)
2. Calculate unit economics (COGS, margin targets)
3. Define tier features (must be meaningful, not arbitrary)
4. A/B test pricing page copy and structure
5. Monitor conversion rates per tier

## Funnel Design
```
Awareness → Landing page → Sign-up → Onboarding → Activation → Conversion → Retention
```
- Measure drop-off at each stage
- Optimize the stage with highest drop-off first
- Use exit-intent offers at key abandonment points

## Conversion Optimization
- Reduce friction (fewer form fields, social login)
- Social proof at decision points (testimonials, user counts)
- Urgency/scarcity (limited-time, seats remaining)
- Risk reversal (free trial, money-back guarantee)
