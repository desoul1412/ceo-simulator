# Compliance & Legal Basics

## Terms of Service
- Service description and acceptable use
- User obligations and prohibited activities
- Intellectual property ownership
- Limitation of liability
- Termination and account deletion
- Governing law and dispute resolution

## Privacy Policy
- Data collected and purposes
- Third-party sharing and processors
- User rights (access, deletion, portability)
- Cookie usage and tracking
- Data retention periods
- Contact information for privacy inquiries

## GDPR / CCPA Essentials
- Lawful basis for data processing
- Consent management (opt-in, not pre-checked)
- Right to erasure (delete user data on request)
- Data breach notification procedures (72 hours for GDPR)
- Data processing agreements with vendors

## Data Handling
- Encrypt at rest and in transit
- Minimize data collection (only what's needed)
- Access controls (principle of least privilege)
- Audit logging for data access
- Regular security reviews
