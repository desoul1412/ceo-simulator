# SEO & Organic Growth

## Technical SEO
- Site architecture and URL structure
- Meta tags, schema markup (JSON-LD)
- Core Web Vitals optimization
- Mobile responsiveness checks
- Sitemap and robots.txt configuration

## Content SEO
- Keyword research with volume/difficulty analysis
- Topic clusters with pillar pages
- Content gap analysis vs competitors
- Internal linking strategy
- Featured snippet optimization

## Off-Page SEO
- Link building via guest posts, partnerships
- Brand mentions and digital PR
- Social signal amplification

## Measurement
- Organic traffic and keyword rankings
- Click-through rates from SERPs
- Domain authority growth
- Conversion from organic traffic
